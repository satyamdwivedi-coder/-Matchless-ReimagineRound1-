Can external visitors access a company site?

Yes. People outside your company can access your site, even without a Google Workspace account. You can also opt to restrict access through sharing settings new domain'. we'll then guide you through.

Can I change the look and feel of my site?

Yes. You can use pre-made themes created by Google or you can choose to create your own theme, where you can customise site properties such as images, fonts, buttons, menu layouts and more. To learn more about creating custom themes, please review this Help Centre .

Can I purchase a domain through Google?

Google can help you purchase a domain through one of our domain host partners. During sign up, simply select the option to 'Buy a new domain'. We'll then guide you through the process to help you set up Google Workspace for your new domain.
